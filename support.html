Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Glōw → Cancel.

How do I restore my purchase?
Open Glōw → tap the paywall → tap "Restore Purchase."

How do I delete my data?
Delete the app from your device. All data is stored locally and will be removed.
