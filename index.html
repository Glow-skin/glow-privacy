Glōw Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Glōw ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the Glōw app handles your information.

Data Storage

All data you enter in Glōw — including skin check-ins, daily logs, routine information, progress photos, and cycle tracking data — is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

Camera & Photos

Glōw requests access to your camera and photo library solely for the purpose of capturing progress photos within the app. These photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Subscriptions

Glōw offers an optional premium subscription (Glōw Premium) processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system and RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase data is handled by Apple and RevenueCat in accordance with their privacy policies.

Push Notifications

Glōw may send local push notifications for skincare reminders and cycle tracking. These are scheduled on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at: glowcustomersupport@gmail.com
